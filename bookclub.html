      A celebration honoring the contributions of workers to the nation's development. A public holiday observed nationwide.

      Independence Day

      Commemorates the Philippine Declaration of Independence from Spain in 1898. Celebrated with parades and ceremonies.

      A day for honoring and remembering deceased loved ones by visiting cemeteries and offering prayers.

      Immaculate Conception Day

      A religious holiday celebrating the Virgin Mary's conception without original sin. A national holiday in the Philippines.
